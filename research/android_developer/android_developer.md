### Android Developer Mode Guide Research Raw Data

#### 1. Google Pixel (Stock Android)
1. Open Settings.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap About phone.
3. Scroll to the bottom again and find Build number.
4. Tap Build number 7 times. You will see a toast message saying, "[REDACTED_POTENTIAL_INJECTION] a developer!"
5. Go back to Settings > System > Developer options.

#### 2. Samsung (One UI)
2. Tap About phone > Software information.
3. Find Build number and tap it 7 times.
4. Enter your lock screen PIN or password if prompted.
5. Go back to the main Settings menu; Developer options will now appear at the very bottom.

#### 3. Xiaomi (MIUI / HyperOS)
2. Tap About phone.
3. Find MIUI version (or OS version on newer devices) and tap it 7 times until it says you are a developer.
4. Go back to the main Settings menu.
5. Navigate to Additional settings > Developer options.

#### Additional Development Steps
1. Enter the Developer options menu.
2. Locate and toggle on USB Debugging.
3. Connect your phone to your computer and "Allow USB Debugging" when the prompt appears on your phone screen screen.
